How to play

Move: ← → or A/D. Jump: Space, W, or ↑. Double-tap jump for a little air-puff!

Collect Lafoofoos coins and reach the glowing goal at the end of each level.

Bounce on top of soft enemies to bop them. Side-touches cost a heart!

Spend Lafoofoos in the Shop on mystery steamer boxes 📦 — each one contains a random dumpling friend! Higher tier boxes have better odds of rare and legendary pulls. Duplicates give back some Lafoofoos as shards. The shop also has baskets, hats, sparkle trails and themes you can buy directly.

On mobile, use the on-screen buttons. Tap Jump again in midair for a small float.
